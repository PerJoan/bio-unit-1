Hello World!
Generally, I would not be sharing anything about myself online, much less post a picture because I am very private.
Seeing that this will not be published, I can be at eased.
My family and friends consider me as a fun-loving, sometimes daring and adventurous individual.
Every year, I look forward to white water rafting on the Delaware River, kayaking on the Long Island Sound and hiking in the fall when the leaves are changing colors.
I enjoy giving back to my community and believe it should be a part of everyone lifestyle.
Currently, I am on the board/committee of the Future Society Inc., an organization which exposes inner city youths to what's lies beyond their four corners.
I am looking forward to attending our upcoming Annual Globe Awards Dinner which will enable us to raise funds to sponsor ongoing youth activities.
